How it works

A clean workflow from ownership to buyer verification

01

Owners add vehicles

Vehicle owners create an account, register their cars, and track service history from their dashboard.

02

Service centre records maintenance

Approved centres add service work, mileage, parts, costs, and verified maintenance notes.

03

Owner shares a report

The owner creates a public report link that exposes only the stored vehicle history.

04

Buyer views confidence data

Buyers inspect vehicle identity, service dates, mileage, centres, and report status before deciding.

Built for every workflow

Owners, centres, buyers, and admins each get a clear path.

For Vehicle Owners

Register vehicles, monitor maintenance history, and generate controlled report links when selling or verifying a car.

For Service Providers

Work under an approved centre, search by registration, and add verified service records without seeing every car by default.

For Buyers

Open a shared report without logging in and inspect the vehicle timeline before committing to a purchase.

For Admins

Approve centres, monitor users, review vehicles, and keep the platform data clean and trustworthy.

For service centres

Run service history as a centre, not a single mechanic account.

Each record belongs to the approved centre, so the history remains tied to the business even when staff changes. Centre admins can view vehicles serviced at their centre and search outside vehicles by registration number.

About the system

A maintenance-first verification layer for vehicle ownership.

The system keeps service history organized around owners, vehicles, approved centres, and public reports. It is designed to reduce uncertainty when servicing, selling, or buying a vehicle by making maintenance records easier to store and verify.

Public buyer access

Buyers can open a report link or enter a report code without creating an account.

Centre accountability

Service records identify the provider or centre behind each maintenance event.

Owner control

Owners decide which reports to generate and can deactivate shared access.
